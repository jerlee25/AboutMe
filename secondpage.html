Let's Talk About CIPHERS

Once upon a time, people decided they didn't like other people reading their stuff.

So, they started making things called ciphers!

There are a lot of ciphers, some of my favorites being:

  • The Pigpen Cipher!
  • The Playfair Cipher!
  • Bacon's Cipher!

What's So Cool About Ciphers?

Ciphers are great because they can be used for so many things like,

  1. Making messages for your friends!
  2. Hiding secret notes only you can read!
  3. Planning to assassinate a member of your family!

But don't just take my word for it, take the word of Mary Queen of Scots, an avid enjoyer of ciphers, particularly for purpose #3!

"I just love using ciphers to chat with my dearest pals across the country!
I can speak with them about whatever my heart desires, even my intent to murder my cousin!
If people found out what I was saying, they would chop off my head, and that wouldn't do at all!
Fortunately, I can rest easy knowing that my ciphers will make sure no one ever unearths my machinations!" - Mary Queen of Scots, 2022

Wow! What a genuine review from a lovely person!
